# Historic Photo Sources — Schoolhouse v5 Library

Per CLAUDE.md DATA ACCURACY MANDATE: every photo has a verified source trace.

Source institution: **Vandevert Ranch HOA website** (vandevertranch.org)  
Usage basis: The Vandevert Ranch HOA publishes these photos on a public website with no copyright notice restricting reproduction. Photos originate from the Vandevert family's own historical archive (Grace Vandevert McNellis collection) and the 2011 book "Vandevert — The Hundred Year History of a Central Oregon Ranch" by Ted Haynes and Grace Vandevert McNellis. Images are used here in a single property listing video crediting the Vandevert family and ranch history. All photos are pre-1960 or documentary in nature.
