# Ryan Realty: Social Media Content Creation Skill

## When to Use
Use this skill when Matt asks to create, write, draft, or plan social media content — posts, captions, Reel scripts, Stories, carousels, or any content for Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook.

## Before Starting
Read `Ryan_Realty_Marketing_Intelligence.md` (BRAND MANAGER folder) — specifically the "Social Media" section for algorithm mechanics and hook structures.

---

## Ryan Realty Content Pillars
Every piece of content belongs to one of these pillars. If it doesn't fit, reconsider whether to create it.

1. **Education** — Market tips, buying/selling process, mortgage basics, what agents actually do
2. **Local Lifestyle** — Bend neighborhoods, outdoor culture, what life in Central Oregon looks like
3. **Market Commentary** — Data-driven takes on current conditions (never hype)
4. **Personality / Social Proof** — Matt's voice, behind-the-scenes, client stories, wins
5. **Listings** — Property content (never salesy — let the property speak)

**Content Mix Target**: 60% Education + Lifestyle + Market Commentary / 40% Listings + Social Proof

---

## The Non-Negotiables
Before writing any piece of content, apply these:

**1. Hook First, Everything Else Second**
The first line/frame must stop the scroll. Every post needs one of:
- Question Hook: "Would you buy this house?"
- Curiosity Gap: "This neighborhood is underpriced — here's the data"
- Bold Take: "Most agents price listings wrong. Here's why."
- Breaking News: "JUST LISTED: Acreage property in Tumalo"
- Personal/Vulnerable: "I made a mistake with this listing — here's what I learned"

**2. DM CTA on Every Post**
Every Instagram post ends with a DM call-to-action:
- "DM me 'strategy'" (for market/education content)
- "DM me 'tour'" (for listing content)
- "DM me 'Bend'" (for neighborhood/lifestyle content)
- "DM me 'guide'" (for buyer/seller education)

**3. Never Salesy**
If the copy sounds like a pitch → rewrite. The goal is to serve first and sell through trust. The word "DM" is about algorithm signal, not about selling.

**4. Always in Matt's Voice**
Direct, knowledgeable, warm, conversational. Like a trusted friend who happens to be an expert. Not corporate. Not cliché. Reads like a real person wrote it.

## Content Formats & Specifications

### Instagram Reels (Primary growth engine)
- **Script structure**: Hook (3 sec) → Tension/Value (middle) → Resolution/CTA (last 5 sec)
- **Length**: 15-90 seconds of spoken content
- **Best performing formats**: Property tours, market data reveals, neighborhood walkthroughs, "things I tell every buyer/seller"
- **Captions**: Include DM CTA, 2-3 sentence hook recap, 10-15 hashtags (location + niche + lifestyle)

### Instagram Stories
- **Purpose**: Behind-the-scenes, real-time updates, listing reveals, polls, market quick-takes
- **Format**: 3-7 frames per story sequence
- **Text overlay**: Short, scannable (5-7 words max per frame)
- **Always**: Swipe-up link or "DM me" sticker if available

### Instagram Static Post (Feed)
- **Hook**: First line must work as a standalone sentence that creates curiosity
- **Body**: 3-5 sentences with clear narrative
- **Specs**: Property: 4:5 ratio (1080x1350). Lifestyle: square or 4:5.
- **Hashtags**: 10-15, placed in caption (not first comment — algorithm change)

### Facebook
- More text-friendly than Instagram
- Include full property addresses (local search intent)
- Can include full market data (Facebook audience skews older, information-seeking)
- Link posts are acceptable (lower organic reach but higher external traffic)
- No DM CTA; use "Message me" or direct call invitation

### TikTok
- Raw authenticity over polish (31% higher engagement)
- Use trending audio when possible (search CapCut's trending library)
- Respond to comments within 24 hours (algorithm signal)
- Same scripts as Instagram Reels can work, but TikTok favors shorter (15-34 sec for discovery)
- Tag location (Bend, Oregon) — TikTok serves to local audience first

## Hashtag Strategy
Always include a mix of:
- **Location**: #BendOregon #CentralOregon #BendRealEstate #VisitBend
- **Property type**: #LuxuryRealEstate #AcreageForSale #PNWHomes #HighDesertLiving
- **Lifestyle**: #OutdoorLiving #MountainLiving #WorkFromBend #BendLifestyle
- **Agent/brand**: #RyanRealty #MattRyan #BendBroker
- **Market**: #OregonRealEstate #HomeBuying #RealEstateTips #BendHomes

Never use: #JustListed (low signal), #DreamHome (brand violation), generic hashtags with 1M+ posts (lost in noise)

## Content Calendar Intelligence
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Best posting days (higher reach and DM response)
- **8am-4pm Bend time**: Optimal posting window
- **Minimum**: 4 Reels/week for consistent algorithmic favor
- **Consistency beats volume**: 4/week every week beats 10/week then silence
